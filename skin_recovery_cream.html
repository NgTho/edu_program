More than soothing.

  • SUPPORT SKIN STRUCTURE

    Collagen is key to growth and repair of your skin. The Skin Recovery Cream uses a unique blend of bamboo, pea, and glucosamine proven to support collagen synthesis. It’s more than just soothing, it’s helping build up your skin.

  • SUPER MOISTURIZERS

    Radiation, sun exposure, and eczema all have the tendency to cause extreme dry skin. Hyaluronic Acid increases the skin’s ability to retain water keeping your skin hydrated.

  • KEY BOTANICAL INGREDIENTS

    Support and nourish your skin with effective botanical ingredients such as Calendula and Aloe Vera. Both of which have been proven to be effective for patients going through radiation therapy.

  • MORE THAN RADIATION

    The Skin Recovery Cream was developed for patients going through radiation in mind. However, we’ve found (and heard from many customers) that it is helpful for after-sun care, eczema, and other similar skin concerns.

Why do I need a Radiation Relief Cream?

Radiation therapy can be very damaging to your skin. At some point during treatment most patients will experience uncomfortable side effects such as redness, burning, itching, and soreness. However, the most dangerous side effect of radiation therapy is a pause in treatment due to skin irritation. That’s why we created the Skin Recovery Cream!


It’s about more than soothing your skin or simply keeping it moisturized. It’s even more than just supporting your skin throughout treatment so you don’t miss scheduled sessions. We’re here to help you maintain your skin health before, during and after treatment. Our hope is that we help make your path to remission more smooth by caring for your skin.
