More than a moisturizer.

  • MORE THAN MOISTURIZING

    Cocoa Butter. Check! Shea Butter. Check! The Stretch Mark Cream gives you those key ingredients and more with polypeptides that help stimulate collagen production and ceramides that prevent collagen breakdown.

  • SAFE FOR NEW MOMS

    As a new mom, there’s a good chance your skin will be changing. Prepare your skin for upcoming growth with a paraben-free formula, safe to use after the first trimester of your pregnancy.

  • OBGYN-RECOMMENDED

    It’s doctor approved! Rejuvaskin Stretch Mark Cream is recommended by physicians and sold in pharmacies all over the world. It’s used by mothers of all skin types to help prevent and reduce the appearance of stretch marks.

  • ANY STRETCH MARKS

    Don’t let the pink fool you, this cream is not just for new moms. It’s great for growing teens, weight lifters, or anyone experiencing rapid skin changes resulting in stretch marks.

Why use the Stretch Mark Cream?

Stretch marks are caused by the stretching of the skin during any period of rapid growth. They are often caused by pregnancy, weight change, strength training, or growth spurts. Stretch marks are a reminder of your journey and what you’ve been through. We always encourage you to embrace your skin health and be confident in it. However, if stretch marks are causing you to lose confidence, the Stretch Mark Cream could be your solution!


It’s more than erasing stretch marks. It’s about improving how you feel about your skin, taking back your confidence, and embracing the journey that brought you here.
