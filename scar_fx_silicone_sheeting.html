Scar Fx Silicone Scar Sheets

A Comprehensive Scar Management System

Scar Fx Silicone Sheets are made from 100% medical grade silicone. Scar Fx is durable, self-adhering, and contours easily to any part of the body. Each scar sheet can be washed and reused for continued use. Silicone sheeting is the gold standard in scar management and enhances the natural scar healing process to soften and flatten raised scars. Scar Fx is solution for scar management recommended by plastic surgeons and dermatologists worldwide.

The International Advisory Panel on Scar Management supports silicone gel sheeting as the primary treatment for surgical and traumatic scars. Scar Fx is effective for any scar, especially keloids and hypertrophic scars.
