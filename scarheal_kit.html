Scar Heal Kit

A Comprehensive Scar Management System

The Scar Heal Kit gives you the best result for your scar with around the clock scar management. Each kit includes our silicone sheeting and silicone gel to be used day and night to improve the appearance of your scar. This combination therapy helps maintain the ideal scar healing environment. Once your scar is flat and smooth, use our scar cream, included in the Scar Heal Kit Plus Scar Esthetique, to target any remaining discoloration.

Our Recommended Scar Management System

Scar Fx at Night

At night place Scar Fx Silicone Sheeting over your clean and dry scar. The occlusive barrier provided by the silicone scar sheet helps create an ideal healing environment for your scar.

RejûvaSil during the Day

During the day apply RejûvaSil Silicone Scar Gel 2-3 times to help maintain the healing environment. RejûvaSil also helps relieve itchiness and discomfort associated with scars.

Scar Esthetique for Discoloration

Once your scar is flat and smooth, apply Scar Esthetique 3-4 times daily to target remaining discoloration. Many patients see continued results through longterm use of Scar Esthetique.

The Scar Heal Kit provides a two phase system for scar management. Phase 1 focuses on the texture and elevation of your scar with silicone sheeting and silicone gel. Phase 2 targets discoloration with Scar Esthetique.

Each product used in the Scar Heal Kit is a silicone-based scar management product. We use silicone because it is the gold standard in scar reduction for even the most severe scars.

The Scar Heal Kit utilizes products recommended by dermatologists and plastic surgeons in over 60 countries around the world. These products are safe for all skin types and proven effective for scar management.
